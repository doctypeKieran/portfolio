About

Junior Software Developer who has completed a Full Stack Software Development bootcamp covering HTML, CSS, JavaScript & Python. This was completed after being a self-taught software developer since August 2022. I’ve got a strong passion for programming and have since acquired proficient experience in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, Python, Django, and React. I previously worked as an exterior cleaner for 13 years where I developed strong communication, customer service, and teamworking skills. I’m both excited and determined to step into the tech industry and work within a collaborative team creating exciting projects!
